How to Apply

Applications are ongoing. Deadline for submission of form is September 1st 2024

Secondary School Students

Eligibility

1. Be a student of a selected public Nigerian secondary school.
2. Maintain a term grade of 75% and above.
3. Be of good character as assessed by the school authorities.

Apply Here

Tertiary Students

Eligibility

1. Be a domestic staff of the Villa.
2. Hold an offer of admission to a public Nigerian University.
3. Maintain a CGPA of 3.7 and above on a 5.0 scale.
4. Demonstrate reputable character of service.
